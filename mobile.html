Photography

One of the reasons I love photography is because it is extremely easy to comprehend but difficult to master. Since I was young, I have always enjoyed taking photos of various different things. With my photography skills, I decided to join the Mattawan Middle School Yearbook club in 7th grade and enjoyed it so much that I ended up joining Mattawan High School's yearbook my sophomore year.

During my time in the Mattawan Middle School Yearbook, I helped take photos of many different sporting events and uploaded them so I could help design the pages accordingly. Although the Middle School Yearbook wasn't nearly as fancy as the high school's, this experience was a lot of fun and introduced me to what designing a yearbook was like.

Mattawan High School's yearbook was a lot more advanced. Instead of being a club, it was a whole class. Pages started to look a lot nicer and we had to pay closer attention to detail when designing. This allowed me to improve my artistic abilities and think outside the box when designing spreads.

Hopefully, in my future, I will be able to continue to use my photography skills from time-to-time to continue to improve my skills and take photos of things I enjoy.
